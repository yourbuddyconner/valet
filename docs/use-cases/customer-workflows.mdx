---
title: Customer Workflows
description: Use Valet for research, account details, support follow-up, and connected customer work.
---

Valet can help with customer-facing work when the task needs information from multiple systems and a clear handoff back to a human. It can gather details, prepare drafts, summarize state, and run approved actions through connected tools.

Typical work includes:

- Researching account details before a customer conversation.
- Summarizing open issues, recent messages, and relevant workspace activity.
- Drafting follow-up responses or internal notes.
- Creating tasks in Linear, Notion, GitHub, or another connected system.
- Running repeatable workflows after a form submission or connected-system update.
- Escalating approval-sensitive steps to the right person.

## Product surfaces involved

- [Connect tools](/product/tools) connects the tools and data Valet can use.
- [Skills and personas](/product/skills-and-personas) shape the behavior for a customer-facing workflow.
- [Access and sharing](/product/access) controls who can connect accounts and share tasks.
- [Approvals](/product/approvals) protect changes and external actions.
- [Channels](/product/channels) send customer-workflow updates back to the right place.

<Warning>
  Treat customer-facing actions as approval-sensitive until your team rules say otherwise.
</Warning>
