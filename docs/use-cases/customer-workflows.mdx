# Customer Workflows

Valet can help with customer-facing work when the task needs context from multiple systems and a clear handoff back to a human. It can gather information, prepare drafts, summarize state, and execute approved actions through connected tools.

Typical work includes:

- Researching account context before a customer conversation.
- Summarizing open issues, recent messages, and relevant workspace activity.
- Drafting follow-up responses or internal notes.
- Creating tasks in Linear, Notion, GitHub, or another connected system.
- Running repeatable workflows after a form submission or webhook.
- Escalating approval-sensitive steps to the right person.

## Product surfaces involved

- [Integrations](/product/integrations) expose the tools and data Valet can use.
- [Skills and personas](/product/skills-and-personas) shape the behavior for a customer-facing workflow.
- [Auth and access](/product/auth-and-access) controls who can connect accounts and share sessions.
- [Policies and approvals](/product/policies-and-approvals) protect writes and external actions.
- [Channels](/product/channels) route customer-workflow updates back to the right place.

<Warning>
  Treat customer-facing actions as approval-sensitive until the policy model says otherwise.
</Warning>
