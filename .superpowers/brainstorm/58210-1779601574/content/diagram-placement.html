A · Drop diagram from execution page
Timeline-only. Add a "View workflow definition →" link in the header. Maximum space for what users care about (results). Diagram lives where it's edited.
B · Compact strip in header
Skinny horizontal strip showing step pills with status. Click to scroll-jump in timeline. Cheap structural overview without stealing space.

Both visible. Timeline ~60%, diagram ~40%. Diagram and timeline scroll-sync (click in one → highlight in other). Most info-dense, least focused.
D · Tab toggle (Timeline / Graph)
Two views in one URL. Default to timeline; users with a "graph brain" can flip. Optionally show a minimap in the timeline view as a teaser.
